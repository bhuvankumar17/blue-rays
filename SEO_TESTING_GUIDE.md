## ðŸ” SEO Troubleshooting

### Issue: Not Appearing in Local Pack

**Solutions:**
1. Verify Google Business Profile
2. Add more photos (target: 50+)
3. Get more reviews (target: 50+)
4. Respond to all reviews
5. Post weekly updates
6. Ensure NAP consistency
7. Add service areas

### Issue: Low Rankings Despite Optimization

**Check:**
1. Page speed (should be < 3s)
2. Mobile-friendliness
3. Content quality (800+ words)
4. Backlinks (need more?)
5. Competitor strength
6. Keyword difficulty
7. Technical errors in Search Console

### Issue: High Bounce Rate

**Improve:**
1. Page load speed
2. Content relevance
3. Clear CTAs
4. Mobile experience
5. Trust signals (reviews, certifications)
6. Easy navigation

### Issue: Not Getting Reviews

**Try:**
1. Ask customers personally
2. Send review link via SMS/email
3. Offer incentive (discount on next service)
4. Make it easy (direct link)
5. Ask at right time (after successful install)
6. Follow up with happy customers
